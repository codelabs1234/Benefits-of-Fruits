Benefits of Fruits

Apple

The apple is a popular and nutritious fruit that is enjoyed worldwide.

  • Rich in dietary fiber.
  • Contains antioxidants.
  • Low in calories.
  • Provides vitamin C and potassium.

Banana

Bananas are a tropical fruit known for their sweet taste and health benefits.

Dates

Dates are sweet fruits known for their chewy texture and caramel-like taste.
